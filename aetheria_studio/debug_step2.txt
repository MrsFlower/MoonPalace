[Paranoia] (Zhang San's level of anxiety and suspicion)
- [Subtlety] (How inconspicuous Zhang San's actions are; lower is worse)
- [Security] (Zhang San's false sense of how safe his silver is)

# Node List

## Node 1: The Heavy Box
**Description:** In his dusty workshop, Zhang San stares at the box of 300 silver taels, his life's savings, and feels a wave of intense paranoia wash over him.
**Choices:**
- [This house has too many ears. I must bury it outside!] (+Paranoia, +Security, -Subtlety) -> Leads to Node 3
- [Maybe I can find a clever place to hide it right here in the workshop.] (-Paranoia, +Subtlety) -> Leads to Node 2

## Node 2: The Indoor Hiding Spot
**Description:** Zhang San decides the safest place is close by, and scans his workshop for the perfect hiding spot away from prying eyes.
**Choices:**
- [Hide it beneath the loose floorboard under the carving bench.] (+Security) -> Leads to Node 9

## Node 3: The Backyard Survey
**Description:** Standing in his backyard, Zhang San sees potential thieves in every rustling leaf and chirping bird, searching for the perfect burial ground.
**Choices:**
- [Dig a hole in the quiet, overgrown corner by the wall; no one ever looks there.] (+Subtlety) -> Leads to Node 4
- [Bury it right under the big willow tree in the center; it's so obvious, it's brilliant!] (+Paranoia, +Security, -Subtlety) -> Leads to Node 5

## Node 4: The Quiet Dig
**Description:** After burying the silver in the inconspicuous corner, Zhang San's paranoia flares up, convincing him that its very subtlety is suspicious.
**Choices:**
- [It's too quiet! It needs a distraction... a sign to throw them off the scent!] (+Paranoia, -Subtlety) -> Leads to Node 5

## Node 5: The Master Stroke
**Description:** With the silver now buried under the willow tree, Zhang San feels a surge of genius as he prepares the final, crucial step of his plan.
**Choices:**
- [Use my finest ink and brush to write a sign: "No 300 taels of silver are buried here."] (+Security, --Subtlety) -> Leads to Node 7
- [Build a fierce-looking scarecrow to stand guard over the spot.] (+Security, -Subtlety) -> Leads to Node 8
- [No, no, that's enough. A true master knows when to stop. I'll just leave it as is.]
